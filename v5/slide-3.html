Where can Ami get his next customer?

Marketing himself
Posted on LinkedIn for months — lots of effort, no customers.
Built a website — no inbound.
Asking for intros
Most intros weren't to customers.
When they were — it wasn't a buying moment.
Paying for referrals
When he received referrals they closed fast — so he tried paying for them.
But his customers weren't interested in becoming ambassadors.
"I'd love to find someone who can refer customers to me — I need a reliable way to get them. Even if it costs money."
Ami B. · Fractional VP of Marketing · last week
Independent professionals have no reliable way to get their next customer.
Sources: 22 interviews; Sales So, Straits Research, MailerLite; Seer Interactive (Sep 2025); Firework / industry data
