The thesis
01
Every professional channel is closing — except one
Cold email, LinkedIn, SEO, newsletters — AI has flooded them all. Reply rates are down. Reach is down. CAC is up 40% in two years.
02
That one channel moves $17 trillion a year on handshakes
65% of small-team revenue comes from referrals. 338 million professionals depend on it. It runs on memory, breakfast meetings, and favors.
03
Nobody has built the software
Every networking tool digitized introductions. None digitized referrals. That's a $17T miss. InTouch is the fix.
