The insight

The value is in referrals, not intros

Intro "Looking to branch out" → "Grab a coffee with my friend"
Referral "I want to pay to solve it today" → "Call this number"
The key ingredient in referrals is timing.

Someone needs to think of you the moment a third party has a need.

Scenario

"Just raised a round — do you know a good PR firm to get me on TechCrunch?"

For referrals to scale, three things have to be true:

01 Scalable with AI
Find willing referrers
02 Known playbook
Keep them motivated
03 Scalable with AI
Trigger recall at the right moment
The more valuable problem has been hiding in plain sight — referral timing.
Sources: InTouch interviews (n=26), product research
