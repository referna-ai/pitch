The Distinction

Intros ≠ referrals

Introduction — "Let me connect you two"
Referral — "Use them. They’re the best."
Introduction Referral
Connects strangers Activates existing trust
One-and-done Ongoing — circle members scan every meeting for your needs
Weak signal — "they seem relevant" Strong signal — "I know exactly what they need"
0% close rate improvement 3–5× higher close rate vs. cold outreach

Every networking startup built the intro layer. None built the referral layer. That’s why Lunchclub pivoted, Shapr declined, and LinkedIn has 930 connections you never talk to.

→ The winning model already exists. It just hasn’t been digitized.
Sources: Lunchclub (a16z-backed, pivoted), Shapr (declined), InTouch interviews (n=26)
