The Wedge

Start with the circle. Own the network.

  • BNI has 340K members generating $26.4B in referrals — but it runs on in-person breakfast meetings. No software. No scale.
  • Our wedge: digitize the BNI circle. Serve the 338M who can't join a weekly 6am meeting.
1
Land in a single profession
Start with one vertical (founders, realtors, recruiters). Build tight circles where every referral is immediately valuable. Let word-of-mouth do the rest.
2
Grow through the circles
Every member invites their best connectors. K = 1.3/month means the network doubles every 3 months without paid acquisition.
3
Expand across professions
Cross-profession referrals compound value. Once two verticals are live, the referral graph becomes the moat — no competitor can replicate trust.
→ The same flywheel that built BNI over 40 years — compressed into software.
Sources: BNI Mid-Year Report 2025, InTouch product data (K=1.3/month, n=48)
