The Distinction

Intros ≠ referrals

Every networking startup tried to create new connections.
None of them activated existing trust.

What they built
  • Lunchclub — AI-matched strangers for coffee
  • Shapr — Tinder-style professional swiping
  • LinkedIn — 930 connections, mostly forgotten
What’s actually valuable
  • A circle of 8 people who agreed to look out for each other
  • Who scan every meeting for your needs
  • Who refer you when you’re not in the room

The difference: introductions connect you to someone new. Referrals come from someone who already trusts you — and knows exactly what you need.

→ The winning model already exists. It just hasn’t been digitized.
Sources: Lunchclub (a16z-backed, pivoted), Shapr (declined), InTouch interviews (n=26)
