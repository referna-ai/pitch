The thesis

Our bet

1
OpenAI won't replace Uber. Anthropic won't replace Airbnb. Both are massive network businesses built by digitizing analog markets.
2
We went looking for a huge undigitized business, and found one: a network of professionals referring customers to each other.
3
So history tells us: a massive network business with great defensibility will be built by digitizing referrals.
4
And that such a business will monetize well through transaction proximity.
5
In such businesses, the winner of the most coveted segment becomes the category leader.
6
Historically, winners had: strong team, head start, transformative insight.
You should not invest if:
You believe nothing huge is left to digitize
You require revenue or a 12-month path to it
