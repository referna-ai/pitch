Referral Network for Independent Professionals

Independent pros' #1 problem
is getting the next client.

Referna matches independent professionals — fractional execs, AI consultants, GEO specialists, and more — into circles of trusted peers who refer clients to each other. The model is proven offline; AI now makes it work at software scale.

The customer

130M independent pros worldwide.

From fractional CFOs to GEO specialists. They live and die by their pipeline — and word-of-mouth is the only channel they trust. 9.26M are addressable in the US; startups are the first segment.

The solution

Curated circles, run by AI.

Referna matches peers into small, trusted circles where they refer clients to each other at the moment of need. Where BNI uses 40,000 manual operators, Referna uses Multi-Person Language Models (MPLMs) — software that runs the circle.

The proof

The offline incumbent runs at $26.4B/yr.

BNI's 340,000 members pay $800/yr to refer $26.4B/yr of business to each other — for 40 years. The behavior is proven. The opportunity is to run it at software scale, for the next 100M.
